Physicalization in Practice物理可视化实践

The Embodiment of Emotions情感具身化

Student Nominated Winner @ SURF 20252025年西浦 SURF (夏季本科生研究项目) “学生提名奖”及“优秀海报奖”

This project explores how to transform intangible human emotions into tangible physical forms. By utilizing natural materials (such as glass and bamboo) and interactive design, we created an emotional embodiment that can be seen, heard, and touched. The project won the "Student Nominated Winner" and "Excellent Poster" award at the Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University Summer Undergraduate Research Fellowship (SURF) 2025. Currently, the project is continuously being improved and refined, and plans are in place to expand the research scope. 该项目探讨了如何将无形的人类情感转化为有形的物理形式。通过利用自然材料（如玻璃和竹子）和交互设计，我们创造了一个可以被看见、听到和触摸的情感载体——风铃。该项目获得了西交利物浦大学 2025 年度夏季本科生研究项目 (SURF) “学生提名奖”和“优秀海报奖”。目前，项目正在持续优化，并计划扩大研究范围。
