AI-Assisted Visual AnalyticsAI 辅助视觉分析

Early Exploration into AI-Assisted Visual Analytics for Dynamic Videos动态视频中 AI 辅助视觉分析的初步探索

Published @ IEEE VIS 2025 (Vienna)发表于 IEEE (电气电子工程师学会) VIS 2025 (维也纳)

This research explores how to leverage Large Language Models (LLMs) to extract and analyze multimodal data from dynamic video stimuli. We proposed an automated insight generation workflow designed to solve human-computer interaction, visaulization and data analysis challenges in complex visual environments. The paper has been accepted by IEEE VIS 2025. 本研究探讨了如何利用大语言模型 (LLMs) 从动态视频刺激中提取并分析多模态数据。我们提出了一种自动化的见解生成工作流，旨在解决复杂视觉环境中的人机交互、可视化及数据分析挑战。该论文已被 IEEE (电气电子工程师学会) VIS 2025 接收。
